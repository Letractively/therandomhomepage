What's this site ?

TheRandomHomepage.com was not intended to be Yet Another Web 2.0 Personalized Homepage. The idea of this website evolved from the fact that everything has its purpose, so as "randomness". Think about flipping through books in bookstore, changing radio station in car stereo, finding an interesting Ad in classifieds section, you might bump into something new, fresh and interesting.

As the point #7 in Google's corporate philosophy points out, "There's always more information out there". The planet internet has lots to offer and tons to discover.

We would like to quote "World Wide Web is a sea of HTML, You never know what you gonna catch"

We have made every attempt to make this site interesting, informative and pleasing. Feel free to give us your thoughts, suggestions, comments or critiques.

Happy browsing,
